TangleTown

Lamps light your way

i How Sim Author

With an app

People choose to participate and share data they collect while walking to places.

Street lamps act as management to sell data and give intimate interactions.

Limitless places

The town square, work, shops, a community park, markets, etc.

Interact

Approach a street lamp to start collecting and selling data.

Street lamps glow slightly when walking near the lamps.

Approach to signal completion.

Data possibilities

Mobile device sensors with an app have various possibilities for data collection while walking.

  • Types of data to sell
    • Urban planning for optimizations
    • Social development analysis for city behaviour
    • Monitoring of infrastructure condition
    • Noise levels in specific locations where people walk
    • Air quality in pedestrian areas
    • Potential detection of infrastructure needing repairs where people travel
    • Statistical behaviour analysis

Accept quests and earn extra

The app could have two modes.

  • Open
    • Go freely to places as pedestrian
    • Paid market rates for data while walking
    • Walk interactively with the street lights of your town
  • Route store
    • Marketplace for pedestrians
    • Browse paid walking quests from businesses
    • Higher advertised rates
    • Incentivise specific locations to walk to

Open mode scenario

1 A person walks to work instead of driving

2 They go outside and approach a streetlamp

3 They have now logged in and start collecting data

4 They stream data to the lamps who glow nearby

5 Lamps sell the data on a marketplace

6 Lamps pay the pedestrian

7 The person logs out of the lamps upon arrival

Route store scenario

1 A person has the day off and does not know what to do

2 They browse and find an incentived walking quest to the waterfront where an event is happening

3 They accept the quest to earn extra money paid by sponsers of the offer

4 While walking they stream data to the lamps

5 Lamps sell the data on a marketplace

6 Lamps pay the pedestrian

7 The person arrives and decides to enjoy the event

8 They log out at a nearby lamp to finish the quest and get paid extra!
