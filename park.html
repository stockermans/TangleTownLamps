Intro Park Sim About

What is this

A Smart Park where Street Lighting manages electricity generated from human movement on smart materials

Resulting Benefits

Start utilizing energy from human movement and pay citizens for it

Encourages and rewards healthy physical activity

Gameify town life with monetary incentive

Creates a feeling of connection between residents and the city

Play by play scenario

1) A person walks into the park with a mobile phone

2) A person registers at a lamp for a timed energy gathering round

3) A person is notified when to revisit a lamp to renew participation

4) Lamps sell the electricity in real time on a marketplace

5) Lamps pay the participants in real time at the park
